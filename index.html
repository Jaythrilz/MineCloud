Our story

Built for developers,
by developers

We started MineCloud because we were tired of overpriced, overcomplicated cloud infrastructure. So we built something better.

Who we are

Cloud infrastructure reimagined

Founded in 2021, MineCloud has grown from a small passion project into a trusted provider serving over 40,000 servers worldwide. We believe powerful cloud computing should be accessible to everyone.

Our mission: deliver the most reliable, performant, and affordable VPS hosting on the planet, wrapped in an experience so clean that deploying a server feels like magic.

Every decision we make — from the hardware we choose to the UI we design — is driven by one question: would a developer love this?
